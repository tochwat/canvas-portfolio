About Me

Hello there! I’m a creative professional with a passion for solving challenging problems through a focus on design and user perspective. I’ve spent the last five years in roles combining my interests in design and technology in order to create effective websites, campaigns, and user-centric content. I’ve led numerous successful projects including a standout campaign created in partnership between my client Microsoft and the non-profit organization Girls Who Code that was featured by the Huffington Post.

I enjoy applying my background in marketing and design to bring a unique perspective to front-end development work, combining my technical skills and ability to work well with a team. My skill set includes proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more, as well as a keen ability to motivate a team through challenging projects with a supportive attitude and unwavering optimism.
